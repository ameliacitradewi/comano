2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account or sign in?

Open Comano, tap "Sign In," and follow the steps. You can use email or Sign in with Apple.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open App Store → Profile → Subscriptions → Comano → Cancel Subscription.

Does Comano store my voice recordings?

By default, voice analysis happens on-device. If you enable cloud backup, anonymized data may be stored securely to provide sync and recovery.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple via reportaproblem.apple.com. We can provide supporting information if needed.

The app is crashing or not responding—what should I do?

Try updating Comano, restarting your device, or reinstalling the app. If the issue continues, contact us and include your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce.


3. Troubleshooting Guide

  • Ensure you're using the latest version of Comano.
  • Restart the app and your device.
  • Check microphone permissions: Settings → Privacy → Microphone → Comano.
  • If using cloud sync, check your internet connection.
  • Reinstall the app if the problem persists.

4. Privacy & Data

Comano is designed with privacy as a priority:

  • Voice analysis happens locally on your device unless you opt into cloud features.
  • We only collect data you explicitly approve.
  • We do not sell or share data for advertising.

5. Subscription & Billing

Comano offers optional subscription plans for advanced features. All billing is processed through Apple. For managing or canceling your subscription, use App Store Subscriptions.
